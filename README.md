**ElimuPay** is a specialized FinTech solution designed to streamline school fee collection and reconciliation for Kenyan educational institutions. By integrating the **Safaricom M-PESA Daraja API**, ElimuPay eliminates manual record-keeping and provides parents with a seamless payment experience.

---

## 🚀 Features

- **M-PESA Express (STK Push):** Parents receive a payment prompt directly on their phones—no manual PayBill entry required.
- **Automatic Reconciliation:** Payments are automatically matched to the student's Admission Number.
- **Real-time Dashboard:** School administrators can track total collections and outstanding balances at a glance.
- **Digital Receipts:** Instant generation of payment confirmations upon successful transactions.
- **Bank Transfer Tracking:** Support for manual bank transfer reconciliation.
